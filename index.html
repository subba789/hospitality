Instructions

Eligibility

  • Sports & Games: Students of any UG / PG program from Technical Deemed Universities, Health Universities, Engineering, and Pharmacy institutes are only allowed to participate in sports events.

Registrations

  • All external participants have to report at the Registration desk arranged near the main gate before 12 noon on 28th Feb 2025.

Registration Fee

  • Sports & Games: It is free for any event.

  • All participants must produce their college ID card and Bonafide certificate.

  • On completion of individual registration, a UDGAM ID will be given.

  • After individual registration, Team captain/leads must report at counters setup for concerned game/ event for registering their team by producing UDGAM ID cards of all the team members.

  • Students registered for sports and games events are permitted to participate in any one Team event of sports and games and any number of Track & Field/ Individual sport event.

  • Participants who require accommodation must register at the adjacent hospitality desk by producing a UDGAM ID card issued upon first come first serve basis.

  • Any number of individuals / teams are allowed to participate from an institute in an event.

  • Lunch will be provided on the three days of the fest for Sports & Games participants only.

  • All the visitors should also register at the desk with a nominal entry fee.

  • Winners and Runners will be awarded with cash prizes, memento/medal and appreciation certificate during the valedictory function only i.e., held on 2th Feb 2025 from 4p.m.

  • Participation certificates will be issued to all the participants at the event venue only upon completion of the event.

  • The team or individual should leave the University as soon as they lost or disqualified for the event.

How to Reach

  • SRM University AP is well-connected to neighboring cities and towns, making it easily accessible for visitors.
  • The nearest major city is Vijayawada and Guntur which is approximately 30 kilometers away. Vijayawada or Guntur has a railway station and is connected to major cities like Hyderabad, Chennai, and Bengaluru. From Vijayawada or Guntur one can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach SRM University AP.
  • The nearest railway station to the university is in Mangalagiri, which is approximately 10 kilometers away, offering easy connectivity for nearby towns. Additionally, Vijayawada Junction, a major railway hub with excellent connectivity to all parts of India, is a convenient option for travelers.
  • From Vijayawada, it takes about 45 minutes by bus or taxi to reach SRM University AP. Local transportation, including buses and auto-rickshaws, is readily available for visitors traveling from nearby towns and railway stations to the university.

Accommodation

  1. Participants who require accommodation must register at the hospitality desk by producing the Mahotsav ID card issued post registration process.
  2. Participants coming from distances more than 100 km from Guntur, Vijayawada and Tenali will only be provided with accommodation upon first come first serve basis depending on the availability with basic amenities.
  3. Participants are advised to bring their basic bedding and electrical spikes for charging their gadgets.
  4. Boys and Girls will be accommodated at different venues; common halls will be provided along with decent washroom facilities.
  5. No additional charge will be taken for accommodation from sports & Games participants; a nominal fee of Rs.100 will be charged from participants of cultural events.
  6. Smoking, drinking and other drug consumption are strictly prohibited and necessary action will be taken by the institute if a participant is found to be in possession of these items.
  7. Any damage to institution facilities and property provided to the participants would result in serious action and necessary reimbursement charges should be paid by the participants found guilty
  8. Participants are required to keep the given check-in receipts and ID card safe until they checkout. the candidate has to pay the registration fee again and get a new ID card.
  9. Participants should report at the accommodation venue to the concerned in-charge with their ID cards every time they enter or exit the room for safety concerns.
  10. Participants are requested to adhere to the check-out time mentioned in the check-in receipt. Check-out after the time indicated will not be entertained.
  11. Participants are expected to not create any kind of nuisance which might trouble other participants in the room.
  12. The college will not be responsible for any damage or loss of property or valuables stored in places of accommodation.
  13. Girls should strictly follow the curfew timings of SRM UNIVERSITY AP that are specified during the allocation of room.
  14. The decision of organizers is final and binding in case of any dispute.
  15. Faculty members accompanying the students will be provided the facility free of cost, if informed in advance.
